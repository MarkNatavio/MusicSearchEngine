About Us


MUSF is a music recommendation application that recommends new songs to users based on their music taste. The dataset of the songs has been divided into four tables: Song table, Album table, Genres table, and Artists table. These tables interact with each other curating playlists according to the users' choices. The purpose of this application is to allow users to find new music based on their taste by providing related songs based on genre or title or find songs too. We are designing the app to combine our database skills and improve our web application design skills.

Navigating Our App


Go to the search page to find new music. Select a Genre or search a song by Keyword. Click on the song of your choosing and add it to a playlist. Loving the playlist? You can make more on your profile page.
